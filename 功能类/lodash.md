## lodash

> javascript基础功能扩展库

![](../images/lodash.png)

**Official：** 
- https://lodash.com/

**Features：**
- ~100%代码覆盖率
- 遵循语义化版本控制规范
- 延迟计算链
- 改变函数的实参个数和顺序

**Git:**
- https://github.com/lodash/lodash

**Demo:**
- http://lodashjs.com/docs/

**Doc：**
- http://lodashjs.com/docs/
